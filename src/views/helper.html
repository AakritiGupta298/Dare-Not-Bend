Dare Not Bend

ML-powered posture detection

Dare Not Bend uses your laptop's camera to analyze and help you improve your posture. Now that you have registered and calibrated the system, simply keep Dare Not Bend open in the background and continue working. Dare Not Bend will notify you if you begin to slouch so you can correct it. You can now close this window, and Dare Not Bend will continue to help you improve your posture!
